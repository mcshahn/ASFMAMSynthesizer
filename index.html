Directions

Waveform: Choose a waveform for your oscillators

Additive Synthesis: Set the number of partials by moving the slider. Click "Play" to hear the sound

AM/FM: Check the boxes next to each title to turn the mode on. You can have multiple modes on. Press your keyboard to hear sounds.

Add LFO: You can choose to add LFOs to each of the synthesis methods by clicking the boxes next to each method. (*Note: For Additive synthesis, LFO is added only to the first oscillator)
